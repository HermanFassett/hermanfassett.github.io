About Me

I'm 18 years old, and currently taking classes at Central Washington University in Computer Science. I've been interested in computers ever since I first started programming in Python at the age of 12. From there I turned to Java and more recently I've been delving into the web with HTML, CSS, and Javascript.

Beyond computers, I've long had an interest in music, both performing and composing. Originally playing the recorder, I first moved to piano and then on to Viola; and I've always loved to sing. Compositions range from a piano piece at the age of 10 to String Quartets, and full Orchestral scores.
