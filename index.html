About


Hi, my name is Herman Fassett.

I'm an 18 year old Computer Science student who loves both music and computers. I've recently been going through the freecodecamp course, and this website is a result of my time there. Before that I'd been programming off and on since I was 12 though userscripts were the full extent of my web development then.
As far as music goes, I've been listening to and playing music for as long as I can remember. Probably my first instrument outside of vocal was a Recorder and from there I branched to Piano and then Viola. I can trace my compositions back to the age of 10.
